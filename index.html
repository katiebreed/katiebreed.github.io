Hello, I am Katie

I am a graphic design student with a focus on visual identity, illustration, and digital media. My work explores how design, motion, and storytelling come together to create engaging and expressive experiences.

I have experience in branding, animation, and creative writing, and I enjoy developing projects that combine strong visual systems with narrative elements.

Areas of focus include graphic design, illustration and animation, and creative writing.

Featured Project

VTuber Branding System

This project is a complete visual identity system developed for a VTuber channel. It includes logo design, animated stream assets, and a fully illustrated and rigged character model. The work focuses on how digital personas are constructed through branding, motion, and performance.

Tools used include Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Live2D for animation and rigging.
