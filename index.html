Hello, I am Katie

Hello! My name is Katie, and I am an interdisciplinary artist with a passion for exploring creativity in all its forms. From illustration and graphic design to sewing and fashion design, electronic art, creative writing, 2D model motion rigging, and traditional physical art mediums, I love experimenting with new techniques and pushing the boundaries of my craft.

I thrive on taking on exciting projects that challenge me to learn, grow, and bring fresh ideas to life. Every new opportunity is a chance to dive in, try something different, and make something meaningful. I am always eager to collaborate, explore new concepts, and continue discovering all the ways art can inspire and connect us.

Featured Project

VTuber Branding System

This project is a complete visual identity system developed for a VTuber channel. It includes logo design, animated stream assets, and a fully illustrated and rigged character model. The work focuses on how digital personas are constructed through branding, motion, and performance.

Tools used include Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Live2D for animation and rigging.
